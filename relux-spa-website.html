Frequently Asked

Questions We Hear Often

Everything you need to know before your first (or next) visit to Relux.

Do I need to book in advance? +

We highly recommend booking ahead to secure your preferred time and therapist. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability, but scheduling ensures you're never turned away.

What should I wear to my appointment? +

Wear comfortable, loose clothing. Depending on your treatment, you'll be given a robe and appropriate draping to ensure your privacy and comfort throughout your session.

Which massage is right for me? +

Our therapists will consult with you before every session to understand your goals. For relaxation, we recommend Swedish. For chronic tension or pain, Deep Tissue is ideal. Not sure? Just ask — we'll guide you.

How early should I arrive? +

We suggest arriving 10–15 minutes before your appointment. This gives you time to fill out your intake form, change, and begin settling into the calm atmosphere before your session begins.

Can I request a specific therapist? +

Absolutely. When booking, simply note your preferred therapist and we'll do our best to match your request based on their availability.

What is your cancellation policy? +

We kindly ask for 24 hours notice for cancellations or reschedules. Late cancellations may incur a partial fee. We understand life happens — please reach out and we'll always do our best to accommodate you.

Do you offer couples massage? +

Yes! Our Couples Suite is one of our most beloved offerings. Two therapists work simultaneously in a private room. Book our Couples Retreat package for the full romantic experience.

How often should I get a massage? +

For general wellness and stress relief, once a month is ideal. For chronic pain or muscle recovery, bi-weekly sessions may be recommended. Our therapists are happy to advise you personally.
